About Us

Your Satisfaction Our Cup of Tea.

Bridging the Gap Between Nature and Technology

Overseas Cyberspace and Environmental Services (OCAES) is an EnviroTech that specializes in providing high-quality environmental consultancy services. Our mission is to support sustainable development through comprehensive environmental assessments and innovative solutions.OCAES offers a unique blend of environmental expertise and cutting-edge technology to provide tailored solutions for businesses and individuals seeking to minimize their environmental impact and maximize their sustainability goals.

Our Services

  • SEIA (Strategic/Social and Environmental Impact Assessment): We can help you assess the environmental impacts of your projects and develop mitigation strategies.
  • Geospatial Data Analytics: We use advanced GIS and remote sensing techniques to analyze environmental data and provide insights.
  • Ecological Surveys (Plants & Birds): We conduct surveys to identify and assess the ecological value of your site.
  • GIS (Geographic Information Systems): We create maps and analyze spatial data to support environmental decision-making.
  • Remote Sensing: We use satellite imagery to map land cover, identify changes, and monitor environmental trends.
