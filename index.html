Your Satisfaction, Our Cup of Tea

Overseas Cyberspace and Environmental Services (OCAES) is an EnviroTech specializing in high-quality environmental consultancy. Our mission is to support sustainable development through comprehensive environmental assessments and innovative solutions.

"We offer a unique blend of environmental expertise and cutting-edge technology to provide tailored solutions for businesses and individuals."

From ecological field surveys to satellite image analysis and AI-powered environmental monitoring, OCAES delivers end-to-end solutions for those seeking to minimize environmental impact and maximize sustainability goals.

🌿 Environmental Integrity

Every assessment is grounded in scientific rigour and a genuine commitment to protecting natural ecosystems.

🛰️ Technology-Driven Insight

We harness satellite imagery, GIS, machine learning, and remote sensing to deliver data that makes a difference.

🎓 Knowledge Transfer

Customized training programs ensure our clients and communities build lasting in-house capability.

🌍 Local Roots, Global Reach

Based in Lesotho, OCAES operates across the region with a growing portfolio of international tools and apps.
