Welcome to OCAES

Overseas Cyberspace and Environmental Services (OCAES) is an environmental consultancy offering a wide range of services.We offer a wide range of environmental services to help businesses and individuals manage their environmental impact.

OCAES is an environmental consultancy and technology business. We provide a number of services including environmental management advisory, Geographic Information System Support, Remote Sensing,Environmental Assessment solutions, Specialist studies(botanical, avifaunal etc) for your Social and Environmental Impact Assessment Studies, Soil survey, landscape architectural design and site planning, permacultural design advisory services,Web development and Geospatial Data Analysis Support as well as customized training on GIS, Remote Sensing and Geospatial Data Management with open source tools.

About Us

Overseas Cyberspace and Environmental Services (OCAES) specializes in providing high-quality environmental consultancy services. Our mission is to support sustainable development through comprehensive environmental assessments and innovative solutions.

Our Services

  • SEIA (Strategic/Social and Environmental Impact Assessment): We can help you assess the environmental impacts of your projects and develop mitigation strategies.
  • Geospatial Analytics: We use advanced GIS and remote sensing techniques to analyze environmental data and provide insights.
  • Ecological Surveys (Plants & Birds): We conduct surveys to identify and assess the ecological value of your site.
  • GIS (Geographic Information Systems): We create maps and analyze spatial data to support environmental decision-making.
  • Remote Sensing: We use satellite imagery to map land cover, identify changes, and monitor environmental trends.
  • Training Programs
  • Soil Surveys
